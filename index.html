Mission Statement

At Equality for pets animal rescue sanctuary, our mission is to rescue and rehome abandoned stray animals in our community, providing shelter, medical care, and love. We're dedicated to raising awareness about animal welfare, promoting adoption, and making a difference in the lives of stray animals in need. With compassion, integrity, and community engagement, we strive to give every stray animal a second chance at a happy life.

About Us

At Equality for pets animal rescue sanctuary, we're passionate about improving the lives of stray animals in need. Founded in 2022 by Sandra Aniston, our charity has grown from a small rescue operation to a thriving organization dedicated to animal welfare.

We're grateful for our volunteers, who dedicate their time and love to helping stray animals in need.

Our Achievements

  • Rescued over 500 stray animals
  • Found forever homes for 300 stray animals
  • Provided medical care and rehabilitation to countless stray animals
