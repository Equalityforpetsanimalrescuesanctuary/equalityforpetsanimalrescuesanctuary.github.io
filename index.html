About Equality for pets animal rescue sanctuary

A sanctuary dedicated to the rescue and care of abandoned and mistreated cats

Founded in 2015, Equality for pets animal rescue sanctuary is a non-profit cat rescue organization committed to saving the lives of cats in need. Our dedicated team of volunteers works tirelessly to rescue abandoned, neglected, and abused cats, providing them with medical care, shelter, and love until they find their forever homes.


We believe that every cat deserves a chance at a happy life. Through our adoption program, community education initiatives, and spay/neuter campaigns, we're working to create a world where no cat is left behind.


Our shelter is a cage-free environment where cats can roam freely, play, and socialize with other cats and human visitors. This approach helps cats maintain their natural behaviors and reduces stress, making their stay with us as comfortable as possible while they wait for adoption.
